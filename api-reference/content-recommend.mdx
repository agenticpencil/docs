Generate strategic content recommendations tailored to your domain, business goals, and existing content landscape using our AI-powered analysis engine.

<RequestExample>

```bash cURL
curl -X POST "https://api.agenticpencil.com/v1/content/recommend" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer ap_your_api_key_here" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "domain": "yoursite.com",
    "goals": [
      "increase organic traffic",
      "improve conversion rates",
      "build brand authority"
    ],
    "existing_content": "We have 50+ blog posts about sustainable fashion, focusing on trends and tips. Looking to expand into new areas."
  }'
```

```python Python
import requests

url = "https://api.agenticpencil.com/v1/content/recommend"
headers = {
    "Authorization": "Bearer ap_your_api_key_here",
    "Content-Type": "application/json"
}
data = {
    "domain": "yoursite.com",
    "goals": [
        "increase organic traffic",
        "improve conversion rates",
        "build brand authority"
    ],
    "existing_content": "We have 50+ blog posts about sustainable fashion, focusing on trends and tips. Looking to expand into new areas."
}

response = requests.post(url, headers=headers, json=data)
result = response.json()
```

## Authentication


## Request Body

<ParamField body="domain" type="string" required>
  Your website domain (e.g., "yoursite.com")
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="goals" type="array" required>
  Your content marketing goals (e.g., "increase organic traffic", "improve conversions", "build authority")
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="existing_content" type="string">
  Description of your current content landscape and what you're looking to achieve
</ParamField>

## Response

<ResponseField name="status" type="string">
  Status of the request ("success" or "error")
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="data" type="object">
  <Expandable title="data object">
    <ResponseField name="recommendations" type="array">
      Array of strategic content recommendations
      <Expandable title="recommendation object">
        <ResponseField name="title" type="string">
          Suggested content title/topic
        </ResponseField>
        <ResponseField name="content_type" type="string">
          Recommended format: "blog post", "guide", "case study", "infographic", etc.
        </ResponseField>
        <ResponseField name="primary_keyword" type="string">
          Main keyword to target
        </ResponseField>
        <ResponseField name="target_keywords" type="array">

        <ResponseField name="search_volume_potential" type="integer">

        <ResponseField name="content_outline" type="array">
          Suggested content structure and key points to cover

        <ResponseField name="goals_alignment" type="array">

        </ResponseField>
        <ResponseField name="priority_score" type="integer">
          Recommendation priority (1-100, higher is better)
        </ResponseField>
        <ResponseField name="estimated_effort" type="string">
          Expected creation effort: "low", "medium", "high"
        </ResponseField>
        <ResponseField name="success_metrics" type="array">
          Suggested KPIs to track for this content

      </Expandable>
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="content_strategy" type="object">

## Priority Score Guide

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="90-100: Extremely High Priority">
    **Must-create content** with exceptional potential for achieving your goals. These recommendations typically have high search volume, low competition, and perfect alignment with your objectives.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="75-89: High Priority">
    **Strong recommendations** that align well with your goals and have good potential for success. Should be prioritized in your content calendar.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="60-74: Medium Priority">
    **Solid content ideas** that support your goals but may have higher competition or lower search volume. Good for filling out your content calendar.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Below 60: Lower Priority">
    **Supplementary content** that supports your overall strategy but shouldn't be prioritized over higher-scoring recommendations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Be Specific**: The more detailed your existing content description and goals, the more targeted and useful your recommendations will be.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Balance Effort and Impact**: Use the priority score and estimated effort to create a balanced content calendar that maximizes ROI.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Implement the Strategy**: Don't just focus on individual recommendations - use the content strategy and competitive insights to guide your overall approach.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Content recommendations are based on current data and trends. Market conditions and competition can change, so refresh your recommendations every 3-6 months.
</Warning>
